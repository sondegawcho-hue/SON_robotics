Find answers to common questions about our workshops

What are the prerequisites for workshops? +

Prerequisites vary by workshop level. Beginner workshops require no prior experience. Intermediate workshops require basic knowledge of the subject. Advanced workshops require substantial practical experience. Check each workshop description for specific requirements.

Do I need to bring my own laptop? +

Yes, participants should bring their own laptops. We recommend Windows or Linux systems with at least 8GB RAM. For robotics workshops, we provide hardware kits. Specific system requirements will be sent after registration.

What is your refund policy? +

Full refund if cancelled 7+ days before workshop start. 50% refund if cancelled 3-6 days before. No refund within 48 hours of start date. We reserve the right to cancel workshops with fewer than 10 participants with full refunds.

Will I receive a certificate? +

Yes! All participants who complete the workshop and pass the final assessment receive an industry-recognized certificate. Certificates include workshop details, duration, and skills acquired.

Do you offer group discounts? +

Yes! Groups of 5+ receive 10% discount, groups of 10+ receive 15% discount. Corporate training packages with custom content are also available. Contact us at sondegawcho@gmail.com for group rates.

Is accommodation provided? +

Accommodation is not included in workshop fees. However, we can recommend affordable hotels near our training center. For multi-day workshops, we provide lunch and refreshments.
